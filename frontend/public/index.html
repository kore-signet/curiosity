SEARCH AT THE
TABLE

An actual play web app that allows you to search for key terms across a whole season of Friends at the Table transcripts at once!

Just select which season of FaTT you want to search through and enter your search term.
You can also click the episode title to go straight to the transcript itself. You can view all completed transcripts by clicking here
